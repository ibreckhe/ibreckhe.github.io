Ian has moved to Harvard OEB

Ian Breckheimer
Last updated on Jan 13, 2018 1 min read

As of November 2017, I’ll be officially starting my new NSF-supported postdoctoral fellowship in the Department of Organismic and Evolutionary Biology at Harvard University. I’ll be based out of Harvard University Herbaria, where I’ll be associated with the lab of Charles C. Davis. I’ve been in the lab unofficially for almost a year, but it’s nice to finally be official!
